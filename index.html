Yongchao Xu

I am a second-year postgraduate student majoring in Electronic and Information Engineering at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Associate Prof. Jiawei Liu and Prof. Zheng-Jun Zha. Before that, I received my Bachelor's Degree from Northeastern University, majoring in Automation.

My main research is around computer vision and deep learning, particularly in human-object interaction (HOI) analysis. My current research focuses on the zero-shot / few-shot learning in HOI.
