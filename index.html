Yongchao Xu

I am a Ph.D. student majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering(ECE) at National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Prof. Robby T. Tan and Prof. Xinchao Wang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's Degree from Beijing Institute of Technology, majoring in Automation.

My main research is around computer vision and deep learning, particularly in human-object interaction (HOI) analysis. My current research focuses on the zero-shot / few-shot learning in HOI.
